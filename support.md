## Frequently asked questions

### How do I play?

- A rotating circle sits in the upper half of the screen. Each pastel segment shows how many dots it still needs.
- Tap **anywhere** to fire the next bullet from the launcher at the bottom.
- The bullet must stick to a segment matching its color.
- Wrong color → you lose. Hitting a dot already on the rim → you lose.
- Clear every counter to finish the level.

Each level adds speed, more segments, and more required dots.

### I lost — what does "Watch ad to continue" do?

Shows a short rewarded video, then brings you back to the exact same level with all your stuck dots and rotation preserved. Only the bullet that caused the loss is undone.

### Can I turn off the daily reminder notification?

Yes. Open the app → **Settings** (gear icon on the landing screen) → toggle off **Daily reminder**. Or iOS *Settings → Notifications → Spindot → Allow Notifications* off.

### Can I turn off ads?

Ads are how Spindot stays free. There's no ad-free purchase at the moment. Banners only appear on the landing and loss screens — never during gameplay.

### I'm seeing "Ad unavailable — try again" on the loss screen

That just means the current rewarded ad inventory didn't fill. Tap **Try again** and you'll restart the level fresh. The ad network usually recovers within a minute or two.

### How do I reset my progress / best level?

Landing screen → **Settings** → **Reset progress**. This wipes your best level and best clean-shots count. Local data only — there's no account to sync.

### How do I delete all my data?

Uninstall the app. Spindot stores everything locally via iOS `UserDefaults`, which is wiped automatically on uninstall. There is no server-side account and nothing to delete remotely.

### Which iOS versions does Spindot support?

iOS 17 and later, iPhone and iPad, universal.

### Is my data sold?

No. The app itself stores your progress only on your device. Ads are served by Google AdMob, which may use standard mobile-advertising identifiers; see our [Privacy Policy](./privacy.html) for the full breakdown.
